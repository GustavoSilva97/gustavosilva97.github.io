Hi, I'm Gustavo! A UX Designer living in Recife, Brazil.

I'm a person with a love for art, pop culture and music.

I've been building myself as a User Experience Designer and Front-End Developer passionate about creating simpler and more intuitive experiences that have a beneficial impact on people's daily lives.

Worked as a Product Designer at Tramontina Porcelana, in a team focused on developing new products and decorations for porcelain tiles in general.

As a student of the Bachelor of Design at UniFBV Wyden, in Recife, Brazil, I gained experience working in a team and learned how design can be transformative in all aspects: political, social, economic and cultural.

In addition to design, I'm a nerd, an avid reader and a storyteller who loves to share stories with friends. Oh, I also loves games!
